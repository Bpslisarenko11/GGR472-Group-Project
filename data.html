Data Sets used


Four different data sets on Affordable housing projects, Hospitals, Subways, and Schools in Toronto were used in this map

Affordable Housing - The "Affordable Housing Pipeline" dataset, published by "Housing Secretariat" was used in the map. Affordable housing projects in Toronto since 2017 that have been either completed or are still in developemt are contained within the dataset. Info that is added to the pop-ups for this data layer is the Address, number of affordable units proposed to built, number of actual units built so far, the start date of construction (if applicable), and the completion date of construction (if applicable). And this data layer appears as the checkbox listed on the map as "Affordable Housing".

Affordable Housing Dataset


Hospitals - The "Wellbeing Youth - Health Services" dataset, published by "Social Development, Administration & Finance" was used in the map. All the listed Hospitals in Toronto are contained within this dataset, and information for this data layer on the map pop-up lists the name of the hospital, and the address of the hospital. And this data layer is selectable on the map as the "Hospitals" checkbox.

Hospitals Dataset


Subways - The Subways dataset was attained from the "Subway Stations" ArcgGIS Online featutre layer, published by "gccagol". This Feature layer was opened up in ArcGis Pro, then converted to a geojson using the "Features to JSON" conversion tool in ArcGis Pro. All of the subway stations in Toronto are listed in this dataset and added to the map as the selectable "Subways" layer checkbox. Information for this layer displayed in its pop-up is the name of the station, the address of the station, and a link to that particular station's website from the TTC.

Subways Dataset


Schools - The "School Locations - All Types" dataset, published by "Toronto Police Services" was used in the map. Contained in this dataset is all of the schools located in Toronto, ranging from Private, English Public, English Catholic, French Public, French Catholic, Colleges, and Universities. Information contained within the map pop-up for this layer is the name of the school, the school address, and the type of school, and this layer is added to the map and selectable as the "Schools" dropdown checklist

Schools Dataset
