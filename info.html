Additional info


In recent history, the Housing Crisis has been one of the biggest problems facing the City of Toronto. For the most part, prices of buying and renting have dramatically increased. This has put many people in a difficult position, particularly lower income families. As a result, the City of Toronto is looking to provide more affordable housing options. This map shows affordable housing locations in Toronto. It also has other locations, like Subway Stations and Schools, which may effect the practicality of each affordable housing project. The user can choose what they want to look at by checking any of the items on the right side of the screen.
